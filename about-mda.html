Message from the Minister

On behalf of the Provincial Government, I am delighted to welcome you to the official website of the Local Government, Elections and Rural Development Department. The Provincial Government has laid the foundations for an effective and responsive e-governance framework and we are at the forefront of implementing that vision.

The Department's website gives a comprehensive account of our policies, priorities and projects undertaken to improve the quality of life of every citizen in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa. I invite you to browse our website to learn more about us and the services that we deliver.

I also welcome your feedback and suggestions on how we may improve our website to serve you better.

SK

Shahram Khan Tarakai

Minister Local Government, Elections and Rural Development

Government of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa

Message from the Secretary

As the top priority of the Provincial Government, my ambition is to provide a local government system that is current, accessible and responsive to the needs of the entire province. Open and accessible information exchange is considered as one of the key drivers of good governance.

The online information gateway has been designed to give you easy and prompt access to information regarding our services, policies and procedures. I encourage you to explore the website and use the information that is provided to full advantage.

We will keep this website current and up-to-date and at the same time request your feedback with your valuable suggestions. I look forward to working with the people of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa to strengthen local service delivery in this province.

ZS

Zahir Shah

Secretary Local Government, Elections and Rural Development Department

Government of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa
