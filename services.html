Services

Comprehensive development services and infrastructure solutions for Sheikhmaltoon Town Mardan

Vision

MDA strives to be an accountable and people-friendly principal planning and development vehicle of the Township, aiming at providing and continuously improving development works, facilities and services of acceptable standards to resident of Sheikhmaltoon Town Mardan. MDA attempts to turn Sheikhmaltoon Town into an "attainable Town" with combined support of people, private sector and different level of the Government, where overwhelming majority enjoys convenient, healthful and satisfied living.

It further seeks to enable Shah Mansoor Town to position itself to meet the challenges of the future and to seize opportunities in an effective, efficient, equitable and sustainable manner, the special efforts on challenges triggered by rapidly growing population, ever increasing demands, needs for institutional adjustments and forces of changes within and without. It is committed to help make the Township to a desired living standards of its residents.

Infrastructure Network Services

The existing infrastructure network services provided in Town are:

Water Supply

Comprehensive water distribution network ensuring reliable supply to all residents.

Sewerage

Modern sewerage system for proper waste management and sanitation.

Drainage System

Efficient drainage infrastructure for rainwater management.

Electricity/Power

Reliable electrical power networking system throughout the township.

Roads/Communication

Well-planned road network and communication infrastructure.

Parks

Green spaces and recreational parks for community well-being.
